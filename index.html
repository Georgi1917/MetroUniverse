Welcome To The World Of Metro

The Metro Universe is a richly developed post-apocalyptic setting initially introduced in Dmitry Glukhovsky's novel Metro 2033 and expanded through sequels, video games, and licensed spin-off novels. Set primarily in the Moscow Metro system, it explores the aftermath of a nuclear war that devastates Earth, forcing survivors to retreat underground. The series blends survival, moral dilemmas, and the exploration of human nature in extreme circumstances.
